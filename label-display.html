‘A Trip to the Top of New England: An Elongated Adventure’ Display Case

Second Place Display, ANA Meeting

c. 2015

photo courtesy of The Elongated Collectors and Ed & Cindy Calhoun

This display won second place at the 2015 American Numismatic Association meeting. Numismatics, or the study/collection of coins, is how many pressed penny collectors describe themselves. Often, avid fans will go on trips simply to find and press as many pennies as they can in a region. This fan traversed the ‘Top of New England’ to gain 13 new Elongated Coins. After their trip, they put together a presentation of their findings, and presented it at a meeting of collectors and fans alike.
