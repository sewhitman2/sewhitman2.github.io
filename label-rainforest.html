Rainforest Cafe Pressed Penny, Gorilla

Gorilla design from Rainforest Cafe, Fisherman's Wharf

c. July 20, 2017 - San Francisco, CA

from the MEC Permanent Collection

The coin that started it all. This pressed penny was the first object in the MEC Permanent Collection. It may not be the most exciting coin, but it represents the allure of pressed pennies around the world. A token of a memory, something physical to represent a time that has passed.

Every recipient of an elongated coin gets to choose from at least four different designs representative of the brand that the host institution wishes to put out into the world. In this particular instance, the collector chose the Gorilla option over a Butterfly, Parrot, or Rainforest Cafe logo design. The machine from which this penny came has been closed since 2017, reflecting the fate of many other machines across the country. Together, we can work together to keep pressed panny machines open and operational around the world!

To see the original Rainforest Cafe San Francisco pressed penny machine, turn around and take the exit into Penny Lane.
